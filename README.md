# GeN

## [Download](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.civeloo.gen)

GeN isn't just an app, it's a digital justice tool that makes it easier for anyone, regardless of their level of technological knowledge or financial means, to manage their business efficiently and issue electronic invoices.
It puts small business owners, who are the foundation of our local economy and culture, at the center.
